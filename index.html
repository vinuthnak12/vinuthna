Production mindset

How I approach DevOps work

Recruiters look for tools. Engineering teams look for judgment. I try to show both.

  • Automate the repeatableIf a task is manual, frequent and risky, it belongs in a pipeline, script or Terraform module.
  • Make systems observableDashboards and alerts should help teams decide what to do, not only show that something is broken.
  • Treat security as part of deliveryContainer scanning, IAM hygiene and safer deployment gates are part of building trust in releases.
  • Document for continuityGood runbooks, READMEs and diagrams reduce onboarding time and operational dependency on one person.
